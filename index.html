About Us

Welcome to Greenville Deli, your go-to destination for authentic Irish flavours and delectable delights. At Greenville Deli, we strive to bring a taste of Ireland to your plate, offering a wide range of traditional Irish dishes and gourmet treats.

OUR STORY
Founded in the heart of Ireland, Greenville Deli has a rich history that dates back several decades. It all began with the vision of our founder, Liam O'Connor, who wanted to share the culinary treasures of his homeland with the world. Liam's passion for food and his dedication to quality quickly made Greenville Deli a beloved establishment among locals and visitors alike
